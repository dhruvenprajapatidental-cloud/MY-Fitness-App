Trainer Rules

  • Warm up 5–10 min + 2–4 ramp-up sets before first heavy lift.
  • Rest 2–3 min on compounds; 60–90 sec on isolation; 45–60 sec on abs/pump work.
  • When all sets hit top of rep range with clean form, increase load next time.
  • Stop if you feel sharp pain, swelling, instability, numbness/tingling, or pain worsening rep to rep.

This Week Strategy

  • Shoulders, upper chest, back width, arms, and waist control drive the look.
  • Legs stay trained for shape, balance, and metabolism.
  • If recovery is poor, convert Day 6 into cardio + mobility only.
  • Sleep, steps, and food quality matter as much as gym sessions.
